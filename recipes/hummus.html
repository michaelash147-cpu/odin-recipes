Hummus Recipe

Home

Description

Hummus is a delicious snack or meal made of chickpeas, tahini, garlic, and olive oil.

Ingredients

l
  • Cooked chickpeas, 1 can or equivalent
  • 3 tbsp Tahini
  • 1/2 lemon juiced
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Ice water
  • Crushed garlic
  • Roasted garlic

Steps

  1. Blend chickpeas in food processor until as fine as possible
  2. Add tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and half the garlic and blend until smooth
  3. Add ice water to help emulsify and blend again
  4. Keep adding little bits of each ingredient until it tastes and feels right
  5. Garnish with roasted garlic, olive oil, and whatever you want!!
