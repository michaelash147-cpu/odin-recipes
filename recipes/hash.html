Hash Recipe

Home

Description

Hash is a delicious breakfast consisting of crispy hashbrowns, either shredded or cubes, mixed with your protein of choice, onions, and whatever vegetables you find tastiest. The best hash comes together in a flash!

Ingredients

  • Hashbrowns
  • Onions
  • Eggs (optional)
  • Bacon, Steak, or any other protein
  • Mushrooms (optional)
  • Bell peppers (optional)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic and onion powder

Steps

  1. Heat pan
  2. Add oil
  3. Add hash browns and let them crisp up
  4. Move hashbrowns to side; add eggs
  5. Once cooked, push eggs to side and add meat
  6. Once meat is heated through, push aside and add vegetables
  7. once everything is heated, add seasonings, mix together, and you're done!
