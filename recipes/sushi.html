Description

Ingredients

  • Cooked short-grain rice
  • Water
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Nori
  • Wasabi
  • Desired fillings or toppings

Steps

  1. Mix rice with water, vinegar, and sugar in a 4:4:1:1 ratio, then add 1 tablespoon of oil per 2 cups rice and salt to taste.
  2. Form rice into desired shape either using hands or a sushi mat
  3. Add nori
  4. Add fillings or toppings
  5. Wrap sushi
